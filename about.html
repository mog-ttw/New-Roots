Our Story

Building Bridges to New Beginnings

New Roots was founded on the belief that talent is universal, but opportunity is not. We're here to change that.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower refugees with in-demand digital skills, connecting them to meaningful employment opportunities that allow them to rebuild their lives with dignity and purpose.

🌟

Our Vision

A world where every refugee has access to the training, support, and opportunities they need to thrive in the global digital economy.

How It Started

From a Simple Idea to a Movement

2020: The Beginning

New Roots started with a small team of volunteers who saw the incredible potential in refugee communities. We began with a single cohort of 15 trainees in a makeshift classroom.

2022: Growing Impact

We expanded our programs, partnered with our first corporate employers, and watched our first graduates land their dream jobs in design and media.

Today: A Global Community

Now, we've empowered over 100 trainees, partnered with 40+ companies, and built a global community of talented creative professionals making their mark.
