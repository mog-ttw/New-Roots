Our Program

A 3-Step Journey to Success

From recruitment to employment, every stage is carefully designed to elevate refugee talent and build lasting careers.

Step 1
🎯

Recruit

We work closely with community organizations to identify resilient, motivated individuals with the drive to succeed in creative fields.

Step 2
📚

Train

Intensive, hands-on training in graphic design, photo editing, and video editing using industry-standard tools and personalized mentorship.

Step 3
💼

Employ

We partner with companies to place graduates in meaningful, long-term creative roles where they can contribute and grow.
